Thesis title: Microbial degradation of subsurface coal seams

    Expected completion date: 2nd of October, 2021

Research breakdown

  1. Identifying components of coal that are biodegraded by microbial communities sourced from the same coal

  2. Determining which microbes are capable of aromatic compound degradation (a major component of coal that is generally considered resistant to anaerobic biodegradation

  3. Finding the biogeographic distribution of methanogenic microbes in coal seams
